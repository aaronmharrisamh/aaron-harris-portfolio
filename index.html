            About

            I'm Aaron Harris, a technical lead and solutions architect who has been shipping production software since 2011. I started building games and tools when I was nine, and that lifelong systems instinct still shows up in how I architect, debug, and lead today.

            My range is end-to-end: full-stack systems, UI/UX, simulation, integrations, AI-assisted workflows, and the judgment to know what should be built, delegated, simplified, or protected from technical debt.

            My pattern is to move fluently between contributor, architect, and team lead: translating between engineers, clients, and stakeholders, turning ambiguous goals into working systems, and helping small teams keep moving when the path is not obvious.

            The work that lights me up has people on the other end of it: watching an audience get hooked on space, or hearing that service techs are home for dinner at 5 p.m.
